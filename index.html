Fighters

HOW TO PLAY

Player1

  • D - to move right
  • A - to move left
  • Q - to uppercut enemy
  • X - to low kick enemy
  • SPACE - to jump
  • Z - to high punch enemy
  • W - to high kick enemy

Player2

  • L - to move right
  • J - to move left
  • M - to uppercut enemy
  • U - to low kick enemy
  • W - to jump
  • P - to high punch enemy
  • O - to high kick enemy
